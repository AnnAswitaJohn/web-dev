The Best Movies According to Ann

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Jaya Jaya Jayaye

This is a Malayalam movie casting Basil and Darshana as lead roles. The movie is set in a village in Kerala around a recently married couple and thier family members. This movie depicts the struggle of the female members in a family as a mother, sister and wife. And how physical abuse from the male members of the family is seen as a normal thing by the society and all the blame is put on the females if they rebel against it.

Por Thozhil

This a Tamil movie casting . The movie is about how a team of efficient police officers solve a series of murder done by a Psychopath. The way the mysteries in the murder cases get reveled in the movie is very intriguing to watch.

KGF

This is a Kannada movie casting Yash in the lead role. This is a movie about a gold field known as KGF and how the hero in the movie captures it and changes the lives of the people who work there.
